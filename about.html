Project Overview: Richfield Connect

Richfield is launcing Richfield Connect,. an internal, web-based social platform designed to digitally transform student engagement. This secure, institution-focused environment allows students to build personal academic profiles, share ideas, and collaborate with their peers. Unlike commercial social media, Richfield Connect is tailored specifically for higher education—prioritizing professionalism, data privacy, and structured academic networking.

  • Academic Profiles Create a Personalized Space highlighting your academic journey and achievements.
  • Peer Collaboration Share posts, exchange ideas, and connect with classmates in a focused setting.
  • Secure Networking Build collaborative networks within a private, institutionally aligned platform.
  • Professional Environment Engage in a structured community designed for higher education standards, free from the distractions of commercial social media.

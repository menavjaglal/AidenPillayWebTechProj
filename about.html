Assignment Background and Case Study

Richfield intends to digitally transform student engagement by launching an internal web-based social platform known as Richfield Connect. The purpose of this platform is to create a secure, institution-focused online environment where students can:

  • Create personal academic profiles
  • Share posts and ideas with peers
  • Engage meaningfully with the student community
  • Build collaborative academic networks

Unlike commercial social media platforms, Richfield Connect is designed as a structured academic engagement system aligned with institutional standards, professionalism, and data privacy expectations.
